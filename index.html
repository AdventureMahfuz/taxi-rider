FAQS

How do I book a Taxi

Open the Taxi service app on your Smartphone. You are automatically located via GPS. The taxis Radar function shows you all available Taxi nearby. You confirm your location with your first click. In ‚Booking Overview’, you can change your pick-up address manually and, if required, enter a destination. You can also specify your individual booking options here.

The next click confirms your address selection and your options, and you bindingly book a taxi. Your driver is displayed with photograph, name, rating and remaining distance.

Do I loose my taxi booking if I close the app?

No, the booking continues even if you close the app. A push message is sent just before the taxi arrives. If you leave the app open, you can track the taxis arrival live on the map.

How does paying with app work?

You register for Pay by App via the taxi booking app: Simply save the payment method of your choice (credit card, PayPal) once and you are ready for cashless and card-less payments for your taxi straightaway. After the trip, a screen with a payment request is shown. You then verify your payment details and decide whether you wish to add a tip. You can also enter a voucher code. After successful verification, you are sent a message informing you of the successful transaction. Your receipt is then emailed to you.
